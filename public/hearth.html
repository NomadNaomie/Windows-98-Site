Who is Hearth Guild?

Hearth Guild is a fully lesbian MC server that was created in order to help uplift lesbian voices.

we thank you for all of the support and hope one day our server will help combat the lesbophobia within this community

aside from being completely lesbian, we are very very diverse !!! we have many neurodiverse lesbians, lesbians of different races and ethnicities, trans and nonbinary lesbians, lesbians who are systems/psychotic—and you can support many of us who are streamers/artists here!
